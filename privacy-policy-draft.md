# Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-04-16

Blackhole is a macOS application that provides a visual black hole simulation experience.

## Summary

Blackhole does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Data Collection

Blackhole does not collect personal information such as:

- name
- email address
- phone number
- precise location
- contacts
- photos or videos uploaded to a server
- usage analytics tied to you
- advertising identifiers

## Local Files

If you choose a custom background image or folder, the file is accessed locally on your Mac only so the app can render it as a background.

These files are not uploaded to our servers.

## App Settings

Blackhole stores app settings locally on your device, such as:

- selected background mode
- computation preset
- zoom level
- color preferences
- helper and interface preferences

These settings remain on your device unless you manually remove the app or its settings.

## Third Parties

Blackhole does not use advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs in the current release.

## Data Sharing

Blackhole does not sell or share your personal information with third parties.
