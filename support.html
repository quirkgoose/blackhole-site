Blackhole Support

Help for setup, performance tuning, and common issues in the Blackhole macOS app.

System Requirements

  • macOS 14 or later
  • Metal-capable Mac recommended

Troubleshooting

The app feels slow

  • Lower the computation mode.
  • Reduce the app window size.
  • Use a lighter background image.

My custom background does not appear

  • Re-select the file or folder from the app settings.
  • Make sure the selected file is a supported image format.

Visuals look different on another Mac

  • Rendering quality depends on GPU capability and computation mode.
  • Intel Macs and lower-end GPUs may require lighter settings.

Privacy Reminder

Blackhole does not upload your selected background images or local files to a server.
